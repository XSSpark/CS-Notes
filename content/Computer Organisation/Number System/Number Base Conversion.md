## Decimal to Binary
---
### Integer
- Keep diving by 2 until we get 0
- Then the binary form counts from *bottom to top*
![[decimal_to_binary_1.png]]

### Decimal Places
- Keep multiple by 2 until we get *1* or *infinite loop*
- Then the binary form counts from *top to bottom*
