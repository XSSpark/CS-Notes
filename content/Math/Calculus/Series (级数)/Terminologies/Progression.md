#calculus 
## Abstract
- Something that can progress from one term to the next

## 3 Ways to define
### List
- Minimum 3 terms to see a pattern
- Mathematical Representation $$
1,2,4,\ldots
$$
### General Term
- Rule for each term
- Mathematical Representation $$
T_{n}= 2^{n-1}
$$
- ``n`` starts from ``1``
### [[Recursion]]
- Mathematical Representation $$
T_{n}= \begin{cases}
T_1 & 1\\
T_n & T_{n-1} \times 2
\end{cases}
$$
