>[!note] First Form
>$$
>\cos(2\theta) = \cos^2(\theta) - \sin^2(\theta)
>$$

>[!note] Second Form (Using [[Pythagorean trigonometric identity]] to obtain)
>$$
>\cos^2(\theta)=  \frac{\cos(2\theta) + 1}{2}
>$$
