## Abstract
---
- Allow 2 isolated [[Process (进程)]] to communicate with each other 
- Used to [[Synchronize]] the activities among [[Process (进程)]] that are cooperating to get some job done

>[!note] 6 types
>1. [[Pipe (管道)]]
>2. [[Message Queue (消息队列)]]
>3. [[Signal (Software Interrupt)]]
>4. [[Semaphore (信号量)]]
>5. [[Shared Memory]]
>6. [[Socket]]
